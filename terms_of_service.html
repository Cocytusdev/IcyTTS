2. Purpose and Scope

IcyTTS is an Android application that converts handwritten input or typed text into speech (Text-to-Speech). It offers two modes:

  1. Free version: includes banner and rewarded ads, up to 3 quick-phrase slots, handwriting recognition, stroke color selection, local TTS voice selection, speech rate control, and basic image import.
  2. Premium version (IcyTTS Premium): removes all ads, expands to 10 quick-phrase slots, enables premium voice catalogs, and grants access to future features.
