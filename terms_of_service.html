2. Purpose and Scope

IcyTTS is an Android application that converts handwritten input into text and generates speech output (Text-to-Speech). It offers two modes:

  1. Free version: with ads (banners and rewarded ads) and basic features (up to 3 quick phrases, handwriting recognition and speech, stroke color selection, local TTS voice selection, etc.).
  2. Premium version (IcyTTS Premium): removes ads, enables up to 10 quick phrases, and provides access to future features.
